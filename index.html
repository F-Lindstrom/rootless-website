About Rootless Capital

Empowering Innovation

Rootless Capital is a venture capital firm dedicated to identifying and supporting exceptional technology startups and scaleups. We believe in the power of innovation to transform industries and create lasting value.

Our investment philosophy centers on partnering with ambitious founders who are building the next generation of technology companies, particularly in the financial services sector where technology is reshaping traditional paradigms.

We provide not just capital, but strategic guidance, industry connections, and operational expertise to help our portfolio companies scale and succeed. We often take an active role on company boards or advisory boards, working closely with management teams to drive growth and navigate challenges.

Our Investment Focus

We invest in technology companies that are disrupting traditional industries and creating new markets

Financial Technology

Digital banking, payment solutions, blockchain technology, lending platforms, and wealth management innovations that are transforming the financial services landscape.

Enterprise Software

SaaS platforms, cloud infrastructure, AI/ML solutions, and enterprise tools that enhance productivity and drive digital transformation across industries.

Emerging Technologies

Artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data analytics, and other cutting-edge technologies that represent the future of innovation and business transformation.
